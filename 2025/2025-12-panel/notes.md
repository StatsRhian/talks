## Future analyst

### How do you think your role will change in the next 10 years? 

I honestly don't know. I've been a bit _AI grumpy_. I can't say "AI" without using airquotes. As a data scientist that's not a helpful. 
If I'm grumpy about AI when people seek my advice, they will seek advice from snake-oil sales folk who _will_ sell them stuff. Leading to AI being done _to them_.
I need to be part of the conversation and shift from AI grumpy to AI aware.
