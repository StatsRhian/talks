About
Open groupOpen group
Got something to get rid of before the big move? Perhaps you need something second hand to save ...a bit of money. Post what you have to offer or what you want to get rid of, just like an electronic car boot sale.
The group can also be used to advertise jobs or support student businesses/ services.

** Rules of usage-
1. Posting spam, fake or damaged goods, or abusive threatening behaviour will get you banned.

2. If you are uploading more than one image, it needs to be in a folder so the home page doesn't get cluttered!

3. If you are on here advertising jobs, you must state the following- the nature of the job including the tasks you are required to perform, the pay type/rate and the governing company.

4. The intention of the group is primarily to benefit the students of Lancaster. Posts which seems completely irrelevent to this cause will be removed at the admin's discretion.


Rules of Entry-
You will only be allowed into the group if you represent ALL of the following-
1) Live or spend time in the area.
2) Represent a real person, a selling page or a recruitment account.
3) You have at least one mutual friend with the admin or one friend already in the group who has association to the University.
